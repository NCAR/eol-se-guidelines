Document Options



The additional options that appear on the right hand side of the document will depend on how the Content Area and Document have been set up.These can include;

Required Read - If you are required to read and acknowledge that you have read an document it will show. Once you have acknowledged the document the Required Read box won't show.

Document Quiz - your score if you have taken it, or a reminder to take it.

Intranet Recommends - other documents that might be of interest to you.

Keyword Cloud - which words have lead other Users to this document as well as the option for you to suggest a keyword.

Rating - either what rating you have given, or the ability to rate the document.

Statistics - information on the documents popularity; recent hits and update details.

Is this Up to Date? - your chance to alert the author to any errors or omissions.

Related Content - documents on the same or similar topic.

STAYING INFORMED Continuing education is important to maintain one's skills, so much so that it's mentioned in the ACM Code of Ethics. Finding better ways to develop software, whether techniques/processes or tools/technologies, is beneficial to the organization. In addition, continual learning is beneficial to one's personal state of mind. Conferences (what is EOL/CDS policy on attendance?), courses, and user groups provide good social exposure. Online resources often provide the most currency. Reading code is a useful and important way to improve skills, as is reading books (and reading books about reading code).
